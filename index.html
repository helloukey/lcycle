What is the Order Process + What's Included?

The NiceCycle 100% Fitment & Quality Guarantee : Since 2008

Each Motorcycle Fairings Kit is Custom made to order and Express manufactured to OEM standards. You can request any custom paintwork or theme free of charge! High Quality + Fast Custom Plastics and Paint work is what you can Expect.

How does the order process work?

1) Once you place an order our Customer Support team will contact you to confirm your specifications and any custom requirements and any questions you have

2) Once project is completed we will send you a number of 'proof pics" to approve your project is exactly as you want prior to Boxing & Shipping

3) One your Kit is carefully boxed and shipped we will monitor and provide shipping details when we receive details from our logistics partner. We are always available at any time to answer questions

4) All kits come with a 100% Fitment , Quality and Delivery Guarantee!


So whats included in Each KIT?

  • Complete Set of Injection Moulded Fairing Plastics to Suit your Specific Model 

  • Highest Quality Paintwork that includes x3 layers of your choice of Paint Combination PLUS x3 Layers Gloss Coat 

  • Professionally Installed Pre-Drilled Mounting Holes, Ready For quick Installation

  • Free Professionally Installed Heat Shields to protect Fairings from any heat damage

Each Fairing and Bodywork Kit is "Made to order " As they are Custom Made for each customer typically production time for Each Fairing Kit Project is 7-10 working days ( Curing time is required between each Coat of Paint - Thus ensuring the Highest possible Finish) 

Note: To Ensure we maintain the highest Quality finish all NiceCycle Kits are " made to order" and thus may take a little longer than cheap off the shelf Kits. The typical process includes: Injection moulding each individual order , Preparing and painting each Fairing kit with its Custom Paintwork, Curing and applying multiple layers of Clear Coat ,Careful packaging and shipping .
We use Injection Moulding to manufacture each kit . 

(Note: During Covid-19 shipping delivery times & estimates have been effected. EMS -DHL - FEDEX & UPS are currently effected. Delivery Times once Shipped have a 14 Working days average delivery window. Please contact us for further information " before" you place an order if you have any queries) 

Please note: We carry injection moulding for Most late model motorcycles. However any Motorcycle over 15 years old may not be eligible for Injection Moulding and may require a compression Kit. Please contact us to confirm.
